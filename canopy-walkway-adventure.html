Your canopy adventure begins with a guided ascent up our spiral staircase wrapped around an ancient kapok tree. At 40 meters above the forest floor, you'll step onto our engineered walkway system—a marvel of sustainable design that allows intimate access to the canopy without damaging a single tree.

The 1.2-kilometer network connects seven observation platforms, each positioned to showcase different aspects of canopy life. From the "Emergent Tower" that rises above the canopy to the "Hummingbird Haven" platform surrounded by flowering epiphytes, you'll witness the stratified nature of forest life from a perspective once available only to researchers.

Key Features

  • Engineered Excellence: Our walkway system, designed by rainforest canopy experts, exceeds international safety standards while minimizing ecological impact
  • Specialized Experiences: Dawn chorus walks, night expeditions, and photography-focused tours available
  • Research Integration: Our platforms serve as research stations for ongoing scientific studies of canopy ecology
  • All-Ages Access: While physically engaging, our main walkway route is accessible to anyone with moderate fitness levels
