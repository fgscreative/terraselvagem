As the sun sets over the Amazon, a transformation occurs. Creatures that spend the daylight hours hidden in the shadows begin to emerge, while the forest itself seems to come alive with sound. Our nighttime jungle exploration offers a rare opportunity to experience this mysterious world, guided by naturalists who specialize in nocturnal ecology.

The Experience

Your journey begins at twilight, as you gather with your guide for a briefing on the adventure ahead. Equipped with headlamps and specialized equipment, you'll venture into the heart of the rainforest, where the absence of daylight reveals a world illuminated by bioluminescence and filled with the sounds of night.

As you walk, your guide will help you spot nocturnal creatures such as owls, night monkeys, and the elusive potoo, using both sight and sound to locate them. You'll also discover the magic of bioluminescent fungi and insects, which create a natural light show unlike anything you've seen before.

Throughout the experience, you'll learn about the adaptations that allow these creatures to thrive at night, as well as the ecological roles they play in the forest. The auditory environment is equally fascinating, with the calls of frogs, insects, and other creatures creating a symphony that is both eerie and beautiful.

Key Features

  • Expert Guides: Our naturalists have extensive experience in nocturnal ecology, ensuring a safe and informative exploration
  • Unique Discoveries: Bioluminescent trails and rare nocturnal creatures offer experiences unavailable during the day
  • Small Groups: Limited to six participants for a more intimate and personalized adventure
  • Safety First: All equipment provided, including headlamps, night vision scopes, and communication devices
