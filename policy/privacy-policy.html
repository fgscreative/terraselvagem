DATA PROTECTION POLICY

Last Updated: June 24, 2025

1. INTRODUCTION

Terra Selvagem ("we," "our," or "us"), operated by Produttività LTD, is committed to protecting the privacy and security of your personal information. This Data Protection Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website, book our experiences, or stay at our Amazon rainforest retreat.

We respect your privacy and are dedicated to transparent information practices. This policy is designed to help you understand how your personal data is handled in compliance with applicable data protection laws.

2. INFORMATION WE COLLECT

2.1 Personal Information You Provide:

  • Contact details (name, email address, phone number, postal address)
  • Passport or identification information for booking purposes
  • Payment information
  • Travel preferences and requirements
  • Health information relevant to your stay (allergies, dietary restrictions, medical conditions)
  • Feedback, reviews, and survey responses
  • Communications with our team

2.2 Information Collected Automatically:

  • IP address and device information
  • Browsing actions and patterns
  • Information collected through cookies and similar technologies
  • Location data if enabled on your device

2.3 Special Categories of Data:

Due to the nature of our experiences, we may collect information about:

  • Health conditions relevant to jungle activities
  • Dietary requirements that may indicate religious or philosophical beliefs
  • Biometric data for identification in remote locations

3. HOW WE USE YOUR INFORMATION

3.1 Provision of Services:

  • Processing bookings and reservations
  • Facilitating your stay and experiences
  • Tailoring our services to your preferences and requirements
  • Ensuring your safety during wilderness activities

3.2 Communication:

  • Responding to your inquiries
  • Sending booking confirmations and pre-arrival information
  • Providing important updates about your stay
  • Sending post-stay surveys and thank you messages

3.3 Business Operations:

  • Improving our services and guest experiences
  • Training our staff
  • Analyzing usage patterns and trends
  • Preventing fraud and ensuring security

3.4 Marketing:

  • Sending newsletters and promotional materials (with your consent)
  • Informing you about special offers and new experiences
  • Customizing content based on your interests and past visits

4. LEGAL BASIS FOR PROCESSING

We process your personal information based on the following legal grounds:

  • Contractual Necessity: To fulfill our contractual obligations to you when you book our services
  • Legitimate Interests: To operate and improve our business and provide optimal guest experiences
  • Legal Compliance: To comply with legal obligations including tourism regulations and tax laws
  • Consent: Where you have given us clear consent to process your data for specific purposes
  • Vital Interests: In emergency situations to protect your life, health, or safety in the remote Amazon environment

5. DATA SHARING AND DISCLOSURE

We may share your personal information with:

5.1 Service Providers:

  • Payment processors
  • Transportation partners
  • Local guides and experience providers
  • IT and system administration services
  • Professional advisers (lawyers, bankers, auditors)

5.2 Regulatory Bodies:

  • Government authorities when required by law
  • Brazilian tourism and environmental agencies
  • Immigration authorities when necessary

5.3 Business Partners:

  • Indigenous community partners (for cultural immersion experiences)
  • Conservation organizations (with your consent)
  • Medical services in case of emergency

We require all third parties to respect the security of your personal data and to treat it in accordance with the law.

6. INTERNATIONAL TRANSFERS

As we operate in the Amazon rainforest of Brazil, your data may be transferred to and processed in countries outside your country of residence. Whenever we transfer your personal data internationally, we ensure appropriate safeguards are in place in compliance with data protection laws.

7. DATA SECURITY

We have implemented appropriate security measures to prevent your personal data from being accidentally lost, used, or accessed in an unauthorized way. We limit access to your personal data to those employees, agents, contractors, and other third parties who have a business need to know.

Our security measures include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data
  • Regular security assessments
  • Staff training on data protection
  • Physical security measures at our facilities
  • Secure backup procedures

8. DATA RETENTION

We will only retain your personal data for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes we collected it for, including for the purposes of satisfying any legal, accounting, or reporting requirements.

Typical retention periods:

  • Booking information: 7 years (for tax and legal purposes)
  • Marketing preferences: Until you opt-out or request deletion
  • Health information: Duration of your stay plus 1 year
  • Website usage data: 26 months

9. YOUR RIGHTS

Depending on your location, you may have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request access to your personal information
  • Correction: Request correction of inaccurate data
  • Erasure: Request deletion of your data in certain circumstances
  • Restriction: Request restriction of processing in certain circumstances
  • Portability: Request transfer of your data to you or a third party
  • Objection: Object to processing based on legitimate interests
  • Withdraw Consent: Withdraw consent where processing is based on consent

To exercise these rights, please contact us at hello@terra-selvagem.com.

10. COOKIES AND TRACKING TECHNOLOGIES

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to distinguish you from other users. This helps us provide you with a good experience and allows us to improve our site.

Types of cookies we use:

  • Essential cookies: Required for the website to function
  • Analytical cookies: Help us understand how visitors interact with our website
  • Functional cookies: Remember your preferences
  • Targeting cookies: Record your visit, pages visited, and links followed

You can set your browser to refuse all or some browser cookies or to alert you when websites set or access cookies. However, some parts of the site may not function properly if you disable cookies.

11. CHILDREN'S PRIVACY

Our services are not intended for children under 16 years of age without parental consent. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 16. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

12. CHANGES TO THIS POLICY

We may update this Data Protection Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or for other operational, legal, or regulatory reasons. We will notify you of any material changes through our website or by email.

13. CONTACT US

If you have any questions about this Data Protection Policy or our data practices, please contact us at:

Email: hello@terra-selvagem.com
