Riverside Bungalows

Perched on stilts along a pristine tributary, our Riverside Bungalows offer an intimate connection with the Amazon's waterways. Watch pink dolphins from your private deck, observe aquatic life through glass floor sections, and fall asleep to the gentle sounds of flowing water. Each bungalow features open-air rainfall showers, traditional dugout canoes for personal exploration, and natural cooling systems that eliminate the need for air conditioning while maintaining perfect comfort.

Canopy Treehouses

Suspended 25-40 meters above the forest floor, our Canopy Treehouses offer a perspective once reserved for researchers and canopy-dwelling wildlife. These architectural marvels integrate seamlessly with living trees, featuring retractable roof sections for stargazing from bed, private observation decks for breakfast amid the treetops, and panoramic windows that position you eye-level with curious monkeys and technicolor birds. Access your treehouse via spiral staircases, suspension bridges, or even an exhilarating zip-line entrance.

Terra Villas

Our exclusive Terra Villas represent the pinnacle of sustainable luxury in the Amazon. These spacious residences feature living roofs planted with native species, walls of glass that fully retract to eliminate barriers between indoor comfort and outdoor wilderness, and private bio-filtered plunge pools. Each villa includes a meditation pavilion, museum-quality indigenous artwork, and comes with a dedicated host and personal naturalist to ensure a completely tailored experience. Perfect for those seeking ultimate privacy and uncompromising comfort in the heart of the rainforest.

Taste the Wild: Culinary Journeys Through the Amazon

(01)

Raízes Restaurant

Our flagship restaurant transforms foraged ingredients into sophisticated dishes that tell the Amazon's story. Nightly tasting menus in a pavilion with jungle views, where naturalists explain the ecological significance of each remarkable flavor.

(02)

Igarapé Terrace

Open-air dining celebrating Amazonian fruits and our forest gardens. Breakfast features exotic fruit stations and indigenous specialties, while lunch offers interactive food stations with the morning's harvest, all enjoyed with spectacular canopy views.

(03)

Copa de Árvore

A treetop bar accessed via suspension bridges, offering sunset views and cocktails crafted with house-infused spirits and rainforest botanicals. Sample our 15 signature cachaça blends while watching the forest transform from day to night.

(04)

Experiential Dining

Exclusive culinary adventures in extraordinary settings—jungle feasts by firelight, floating lunches through flooded forests, or private dinners beside hidden waterfalls, combining exceptional cuisine with spectacular wilderness locations.

(05)

Terra Harvest

Interactive culinary workshops where you harvest ingredients from our forest gardens and learn traditional preparation techniques from indigenous chefs. Create Amazonian specialties using ancient methods, from preparing manioc flour to fermenting tropical fruits.
